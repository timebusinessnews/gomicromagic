About Us

t didn’t start in a boardroom with spreadsheets and market projections. It started on a quiet hiking trail, somewhere deep in the lush forests of British Columbia, with the feeling of damp earth under our boots and the shared realization that something in our modern lives was missing. We were chasing deadlines, climbing corporate ladders, and scrolling through endless feeds, but we felt… disconnected. Disconnected from nature, from each other, and most importantly, from ourselves.

That’s where the magic began. Our own personal journeys with psilocybin and the profound, gentle power of microdosing changed everything. It wasn’t about escaping reality; it was about finding our way back to it. It was the quiet clarity on a chaotic Monday, the surge of creative energy on a stale project, the rediscovery of wonder in a world that had started to feel grey and transactional. We found a tool that helped us quiet the noise and amplify the signal of our own consciousness.

But as we explored, we saw a problem. The space felt secretive, inconsistent, and lacked the kind of care and intention we believed this medicine deserved. We knew we weren’t the only Canadians feeling this way—curious, hopeful, but looking for a safe and trustworthy guide.

So, Go Micro Magic was born. It was born from a passion for wellness and a deep respect for the wisdom of fungi. We became obsessed. We connected with dedicated, small-batch growers across Canada who share our ethos—people who understand that the real magic isn’t just in the psilocybin, but in the quality, the care, and the intention infused into every step of the process.

We’re not just an online store; we see ourselves as stewards of a sacred experience. Every product we offer, from our precisely measured microdose capsules to our thoughtfully crafted edibles, is a tool we’ve used and believe in ourselves. This is more than a business to us; it’s our community. It’s our way of sharing the clarity and connection we found.

So if you’re here, you’re probably a little curious, too. Maybe you’re seeking a bit more creativity, a little less anxiety, or just a new perspective. Whatever your path, we’re just glad you found your way to ours.

Welcome to the magic.
